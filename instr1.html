Instructions

Single Player

  • Click on the blocks to reveal their contents.
  • A block may contain a fielder or a score.
  • If you are caught by fielder, the game ends if your score is less than 10 .
  • If your score is greater than 10 and you are caught by fielder then you will get lifeline if you wish with decrease in your score by 10 runs.
  • You will be having only one lifeline.
  • If you click on score, your total score increases.
  • The goal is to score as many runs as possible before clicking on a fielder.
  • Each block can have a random score of 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, or 6 runs.
  • Be careful! The fielders are randomly placed, so you never know where they are.
  • You also have power-ups placed in the cells randomly which you will be knowing when you clicked on that.
  • You also have another option called reveal fielders which you can use only after 7 Balls and runs more than 10. You should utilize this very carefully as it decreases your score by 5 runs and shows only for 1 second and can use only once.
