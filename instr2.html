Instructions

Two Players

  • First enter player names with separated by a comma.
  • Enter the grid size from 6 to 11.
  • Each player should play one by one.
  • Click on the blocks to reveal their contents.
  • A block may contain a fielder or a score.
  • Be careful! The fielders are randomly placed, so you never know where they are.
  • If a player is caught by fielder, the game ends.
  • Winner is the person who is not caught by fileder.
